About the project

zfudbalmk is a personal project created by Marcos Marín Martín, an enthusiast from Madrid with a passion for two things: women's football and Macedonia.

The main goal is to give Macedonian women's football the visibility it deserves. What started as a simple Instagram page (@zfudbalmk) has evolved into this website, designed to make player stats, results, and data more accessible to everyone.

If you have any suggestions, corrections, or just want to chat about football, the DMs on Instagram are always open!
